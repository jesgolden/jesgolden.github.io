Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

What I collect

If you subscribe to my email list, I collect your email address. If you use the contact form, I receive your name, email address, and message.

How it's used

Email addresses are used only to send the newsletter you signed up for. Contact form messages are used only to respond to you. I never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing.

Services that process data on my behalf

The email list is managed through Mailchimp, the contact form through Formspree, and payments through Stripe. Each processes your information under its own privacy policy; I never see or store your payment card details.

Unsubscribing & removal

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. To have your information removed entirely, or to ask what I hold about you, email me here and I'll make sure you're removed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
