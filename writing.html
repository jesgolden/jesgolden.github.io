Writing Mentorship

Get to the heart of it.

I provide attuned support for writers, artists, and applicants drawing on over a decade of experience as an editor. You know you have a good idea even though it's a bit amorphous—I help you take the journey of finding the form, content, and style that will make your project unforgettable. Teaching hundreds of undergraduates at UC Berkeley during my doctoral work taught me to ask rigorous questions that get you clear on your idea, your organization, and your voice. I bring analytical, editorial, and mindfulness-based tools; you get a discerning guide who sees the heart of your project and helps it come forth. Mentorship, editorial, and hybrid packages available.

Grants Proposals, artist statements, budgets-as-narrative.

Grant writing

You are creating work that brings us closer to a more just and beautiful world; I help visionaries on the frontiers and the margins secure the funding to make those projects possible. My grant editing has helped artists secure $10,000+ grants from funders like the Jim Joseph Foundation and the Jewish Liberation Fund. I step in at any stage to craft proposals, artist statements, and project narratives that capture your voice and the soul of your project. Together, we articulate why the time is ripe for your work. Reach out for a consultation.
