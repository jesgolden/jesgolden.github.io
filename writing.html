Writing Mentorship

The questions that bring you back to what you mean.

Attuned, honest support for writers and applicants: developmental feedback, editing, and the kind of questions that bring you back to what you actually mean. I work with grant proposals, essays, academic and graduate school applications, articles, and creative projects—drawing on a decade of editing alongside my training as a philosopher and teacher.

Grants Proposals, artist statements, budgets-as-narrative.

Grant writing

I've helped artists win over $20,000 in grants, including Jewish arts funding. Grant writing is translation work: taking what you know in your body and practice and rendering it in the language a funder can say yes to—without losing what makes the work yours. We work on proposals, artist statements, and project narratives together, at whatever stage the application is in.
