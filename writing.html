Writing Mentorship

Get to the heart of it.

I provide attuned support for writers, artists, and applicants drawing on over a decade of experience as an editor. You know you have a good idea even though it's a bit amorphous—I help you take the journey of finding the form, content, and style that makes your project vivid and unforgettable. Teaching hundreds of undergraduates at UC Berkeley during my doctoral work taught me to ask rigorous questions that get you clear on your idea, your organization, and your voice. I bring analytical, editorial, and mindfulness-based tools; you get a discerning guide who sees the heart of your project and helps it come forth. Mentorship, editorial, and hybrid packages available.

My essays have appeared in Hey Alma and At the Well.

Grants Proposals, artist statements, budgets-as-narrative.

Grant writing

I've helped artists win over $20,000 in grants from the Jim Joseph Foundation and Jewish Liberation Fund. I know how to sense and explain what's distinctive and unforgettable about your project, and how to tailor that language to funders. We work on proposals, artist statements, and project narratives together, at whatever stage the application is in.
